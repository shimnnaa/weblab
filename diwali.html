diwali

Diwali, also called Deepavali, is a major festival celebrated by millions of people, especially in India. It symbolizes the victory of light over darkness and good over evil.

time of celebration

Diwali usually lasts for five days, falling between October and November. During this time, people clean and decorate their homes with lamps and colorful rangoli designs. They wear new clothes, exchange gifts, and prepare special sweets.
